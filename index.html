TicTacToe Game

TicTacToe Game
Description:

Tic-tac-toe is played on a three-by-three grid by two players, who alternately place the marks X and O in one of the nine spaces in the grid. Have the first player go first. Traditionally, the first player goes with "X", second player go second, usually second player goes with "O". Keep alternating moves until one of the players has drawn a row of three symbols or until no one can win the game. In my project, you will see when the winner appearance, it will be highlight the winner as yellow color. Also, you have power to go back check each player's steps and modify the steps during the game. Sounds easy, let's practice it!

ThreeInRow Game

Three-In-Row Game
Description:

For this project, I am fetching data from publicly available API, generate random grid for this game. The size of grid can be 6x6, 8x8, 10x10 or 14x14. When user click one time, the square will turn blue, click on two times will turn white, click on three times will turn to grey. The button "Check Puzzle" is able to check the puzzle's correction during the game;

  • So far so good

    • It happens when the current squares are correctly but the puzzle is incomplete.

  • Something is wrong

    • One or more of the colored squares is incorrectly assigned.

  • You did it

    • All squares are correct and the puzzle has been completely filled in.

The checkbox "show Errors" will show which square or squares is wrong.

  • HTML5/CSS3
  • JavaScript
